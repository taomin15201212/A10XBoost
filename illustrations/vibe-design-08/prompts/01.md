
Title: Vibe Design 范式迁移 - 对比图

Comparison View, 16:9, notion style minimalist hand-drawn line art.

LEFT SIDE - 传统设计流程:
- 线框 -> 视觉稿 -> 交互细化 -> 评审返工 -> 开发交付
- 关键词标签: “步骤多”, “反馈慢”, “沟通损耗”
- visual mood: heavier, longer path arrows

RIGHT SIDE - Vibe Design流程:
- 意图描述 -> AI多方案生成 -> 设计判断收敛 -> 快速验证
- 关键词标签: “先定方向”, “高速试错”, “快速可测”
- visual mood: cleaner loop, shorter arrows

Divider in center with label: “从画得快，到定得准”
Use white background, black/dark gray lines, sparse pastel blue/yellow accents, abundant whitespace, clear Chinese labels.
