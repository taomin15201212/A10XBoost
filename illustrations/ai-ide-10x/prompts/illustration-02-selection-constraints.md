AI IDE 选型四约束 - Conceptual Framework

STRUCTURE: 2x2 decision framework with four major constraint cards and role-based paths

NODES:
- 网络与合规 - 能否使用海外服务，企业策略限制
- 主要开发任务 - 脚本自动化 / 产品迭代 / 大仓库维护
- 模型切换能力 - 是否支持多模型与BYOK
- 验证能力 - 能否从生成走到可运行、可回归

RELATIONSHIPS:
- Center decision hub: "是否适配当前团队"
- Four directional arrows from each constraint into center
- Bottom outputs: 个人效率方案 / 团队交付方案 / 国内合规方案
- Small risk badges on each constraint (e.g. 只看热度 = 高风险)

TEXT LABELS (Chinese):
- 标题: 选型别看热度，看约束
- 角标: 可用性 > 花哨功能

STYLE: notion style with clean modular cards, concise Chinese typography, hand-drawn iconography for network, model, test, repository
ASPECT: 16:9, medium-high complexity
